# [Legacy HUD & Legacy HUD Tweaks](https://www.mousehuntgame.com/preferences.php?tab=mousehunt-improved-settings#mousehunt-improved-settings-feature-legacy-hud)

Enable the legacy HUD or make tweaks to it.

## Options

- **Enable the legacy menu**: Enables the menu of the old HUD.
- **Enable the legacy stats bar**: Enables the stats bar of the old HUD.
- **Enable tweaks to the legacy HUD**: Modifies certain parts of the Legacy HUD. (Enabled by default)
