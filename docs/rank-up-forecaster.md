# [Rank-Up Forecaster](https://www.mousehuntgame.com/preferences.php?tab=mousehunt-improved-settings#mousehunt-improved-settings-beta-rank-up-forecaster)

Beta feature that tracks your wisdom over time and estimates when you'll reach
your next rank.

## Features

- Records wisdom samples as you hunt, travel, and refresh the forecast
- Shows a headline ETA with a progress bar, pace, wisdom remaining, and hunt
  estimates
- Displays an interactive wisdom history chart with projection lines for several
  forecast models
- Compares multiple forecasting approaches to produce a single headline estimate
- Breaks down recent wisdom gains by location and shows your latest stored
  samples
- Imports existing data from the original Rank-Up Forecaster userscript when
  available
- Opens from the Kingdom menu or by clicking the points/title row in the HUD
